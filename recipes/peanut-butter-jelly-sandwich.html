Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

A classic that will surely bring back nostalgic memories.

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread.
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly. I recommend strawberry or grape.

Recipe

  1. Spread Peanut Butter evenly over 1 slice of bread.
  2. Spread Jelly evenly over 1 slice of bread.
  3. Stack both slices of bread evenly with the Peanut Butter and Jelly facing in.
