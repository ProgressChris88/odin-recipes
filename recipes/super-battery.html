Super Battery

In order to stop Purple Tentacle from complete world domination, you need this battery to power the Chrono-John.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Vinegar
  • Gold

Recipe

  1. Ask Thomas Edison to include a bottle of wine in his time capsule.
  2. Get the Gold Plated Quill Pen from the founders.
  3. Charge the battery.
  4. Stop Purple Tentacle from taking over the world!
